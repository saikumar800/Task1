Face recognition Autometic System

Facial recognition is a way of identifying or confirming an individual’s identity using their face. Facial recognition systems can be used to identify people in photos, videos, or in real-time. Facial recognition is a category of biometric security. Other forms of biometric software include voice recognition, fingerprint recognition, and eye retina or iris recognition. The technology is mostly used for security and law enforcement, though there is increasing interest in other areas of use.

The Blood Project

The Blood Project is dedicated to improving medical knowledge about blood’s connection to human health, disease, and therapeutics. It provides open-access, web-based, and expert-curated learning tools and resources designed to increase clinicians’ hematological knowledge, foster critical thinking and investigational skills in medical practitioners beyond rote-memorized approaches to patient care, and boost understanding of humanistic perspectives on blood and blood-related disorders that improve physicians’ well-being and patient outcomes.
